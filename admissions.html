How To Apply

The way you apply differs depending on where you’ve come from, and where you’re headed. We’ve explained everything below.

Applications


Direct application (Domestic)

Direct applications include postgraduate study, honours, or single unit enrolment.

VTAC application (Domestic)

Applications for university are handled through VTAC.

Direct application (International students)

If you’re coming to Monash from overseas, you will need to apply directly to the university to start your Monash journey.

Graduate Research application

Before you submit an application for graduate research, you’ll need to file an Expression of Interest with a managing faculty.

Indigenous student application

Monash University is committed to advancing the education and career aspirations of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people.We have a range of admissions entry points for Indigenous applicants designed to address the under-representation of Australian Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students in Australian universities.
